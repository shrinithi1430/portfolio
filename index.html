Who I Am

Passionate about pixels & purpose

I'm Shrinithi, a UI/UX designer and web developer dedicated to creating elegant, intuitive digital experiences. My work merges thoughtful design with clean, performant code—delivering products that are both beautiful and purposeful.

I specialise in end-to-end product design and development, from user research and wireframing in Figma to implementing responsive, accessible interfaces using modern web technologies. I've worked on real-time platforms, e-commerce systems, and sustainability-focused applications—each project grounded in user empathy and technical excellence.

My philosophy: Great design solves problems invisibly. Great development makes it fast and reliable. I bridge these disciplines to deliver cohesive, polished digital products.
